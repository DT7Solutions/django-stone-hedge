History of Stonehenge Granites

Stonehenge Granites ventured into the granite business in 2015 as part of our diversification strategy. We established our first factory in 2020 in Andhra Pradesh. Building on our early achievements, we expanded into granite quarries in Telangana the same year. Currently, we are in the process of acquiring an additional quarry in Andhra Pradesh and constructing a new factory in Telangana. Our operations are complemented by our expertise in IT services through Subhujo Technologies Private Limited, with a presence in India, South Africa, Nigeria, USA and Dubai.

Vision for the Future:

We aim to uphold the highest standards of quality in our products and services, cultivate a culture of excellence among our staff, and expand our global presence to become a prominent player in the granite industry.

    Unique Selling Points:
  • Quality: We are committed to providing the highest quality granite products.
  • Customization: We offer tailored solutions to meet our clients' specific needs.
  • Competitive Pricing: Our pricing strategy ensures excellent value for money.
    Notable Clients and Markets:
  • Domestic Market: Comprehensive distribution throughout India.
  • International Market: Exporting granite blocks to China and Europe, and polished slabs to the UAE, Vietnam, and Turkey.
Our Vision

At Stonehenge Granites, we envision becoming the leading supplier of premium granite and natural stone solutions in India and globally. With a focus on sustainable sourcing, innovation, and high-quality craftsmanship, we aim to transform spaces through beautiful stone installations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality granite and natural stone products that cater to the diverse needs of residential and commercial spaces. We strive to deliver excellence in every project, from kitchen countertops to grand flooring installations, with a commitment to customer satisfaction, sustainability, and craftsmanship.
